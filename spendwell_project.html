SpendWell

Project Overview

SpendWell is an AI-powered finance coach and expense tracker designed to help individuals make better financial decisions. By analyzing users' spending patterns and financial goals, the app offers personalized financial advice and proactive recommendations. The platform aims to simplify budgeting, savings, and investment management with cutting-edge technology.

Key Features

View PDF
  • **Personalized Financial Dashboard:** Overview of income, expenses, savings, and financial goals.
  • **AI-Powered Chatbot:** Leverages the Gemini API to answer financial questions and provide advice based on user data.
  • **Expense Categorization:** Automatically classifies expenditures for better tracking and identifies areas of overspending.
  • **Budgeting & Alerts:** Users can set budgets and receive proactive alerts for potential overspending or insufficient savings.
  • **Savings Goals:** Set and track progress toward personal savings and investment goals.
  • **Financial Reports:** Visual reports that show spending patterns, savings progress, and more.
